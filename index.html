Common Questions

Everything you need to know before you enquire

We've answered the most common questions so you can make a confident decision.

How long does installation typically take?

Most installations are completed within a single working day. A small shopfront sign takes 4–6 hours. A room of smart film or interior wall film typically takes 3–5 hours. Larger projects are scheduled over 1–2 days. We'll give you a precise timeline in your written quotation.

Is the free site survey really free with no obligation?

Yes, completely. We visit your site, take measurements, discuss your requirements and provide a detailed written quotation at no charge. There is no obligation to proceed. We believe in earning your business through quality advice, not pressure tactics.

What is PDLC smart film and how does it work?

PDLC (Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal) film is applied directly to existing glass. When powered off, the liquid crystals scatter light — making the glass appear frosted and private. When switched on, they align and the glass becomes clear. The transition happens instantly with a wall switch or remote control. It can also be connected to smart home systems.

Is interior film durable? How long does it last?

Premium interior film from our TGA® certified range is rated for 5–10 years under normal indoor conditions. It is scratch-resistant, moisture-resistant and suitable for high-traffic areas. Kitchen and bathroom applications use grades specifically formulated for heat and humidity. The film can be removed and replaced without damaging the underlying surface.

Do you handle HDB and residential projects?

Yes. Window film, interior film, and decorative glass film are all HDB-friendly — they require no hacking, drilling or structural changes and do not violate HDB renovation guidelines. Smart film installation requires a power supply but can be wired neatly through existing cabling channels. We handle all types of residential projects across Singapore.

What areas in Singapore do you cover?

We provide island-wide service across all of Singapore — CBD, Orchard, North, South, East and West, including all industrial estates and HDB towns. There is no travel surcharge for site surveys. Whether you're in Jurong West or Changi, we'll come to you.
